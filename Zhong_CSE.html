Project: Image Artist

Course: CSE(Computer science engineering)

Duration: 3/6/18 - 3/26/18

Objective: Use python to edit photos

Type of project: Group project

Problem:

Your client is a family that would like a standard frame applied to a large number of pictures that feature one or more of the family members. They want the composite image to be memorable and to incorporate some personalized symbol, image, or silhouette that represents the interests of the family member(s). The client enjoys abstract art as well and might like to see geometric shape incorporated in the image—drawn on, as a border, or as a mask. The client enjoys participating in the creative process and will appreciate being offered a range of options (as a parameter) for one of the image operations you perform.

Solution:

Our algorithm solves the problem by providing multiple colored frames for families and their pictures. We also provide a geometric figure/symbol to decorate the image even more. By working on making customized frames that are good in any photo, we have accomplished to only bring you the highest quality.

My mainly role in this project was coding, researching, and bugs fixing. The biggest problem for this project was coding errors. Because this is the first python image project and we don’t have any experiences. However, I fixed all the codes through researching and helping each other. I learned not only python, but also team working and communication.
