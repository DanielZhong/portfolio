Project: App Inventor Project

Course: CSE(Computer science engineering)

Duration: 9/19/17 - 10/6/17

Objective: make a game using App Inventor that can play on the Samsung devices.

Type of project: Group project

In this project, our brainstorming idea was the platform game that consist of a character that could jump as a well as move left and right to avoid spikes spread throughout the level. When the character reached the checkpoint, the next level would begin, and we would have around 3 levels in total. After I read the rubric and the instruction, I realized that we can’t edit the code together, so I decided share out the work. I’m responsible for the document part and flow chart. Since the project was done early, the challenge part for us was the daily log. In this project, I learned not only the App Inventor skills, but also leadership and teamwork.
